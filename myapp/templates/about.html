About Moonwalk Threads

Moonwalk Threads is a specialty, curated vintage store located in the heart of Downtown Auburn, CA. Founded in 2020, Moonwalk was built on the love of vintage clothes and sustainable fashion. We are proud to be a local woman-owned small business.

Shop with us in store and browse our 90’s and early 2000’s vintage clothing. You can also find us at our yearly events, operating during the spring and summer. Our mission is to serve the community with high quality, affordable, vintage fashion! We can’t wait to see you at Moonwalk!
